About Us

Olympia Did That is a watchdog-style project tracking bills and decisions that raise the cost of living, weaken public safety, and expand government power — with receipts.

See the Wall Browse the Bills Contact
What we do
  • Track bills in categories like taxes and crime, with clear status badges.
  • Connect the dots between who votes for what and what it costs families.
  • Publish shareable summaries so normal people can understand what’s happening.
  • Promote accountability using public information and documented sources.
What we’re not
  • We’re not a government agency.
  • We’re not your lawyer. (Nothing here is legal advice.)
  • We’re not perfect — and we welcome corrections.
